About Us

Discover How You can make Payment Smarter!


Karrabo is a CBN licensed PTSP (Payment Terminal Service Provider) whose focus is on providing platforms and tools to promote trade and commerce. We leverage technology to provide solutions that deliver the same kind of banking information in the banking hall at your doorstep . Karrabo is a financial solution provider with a focus on the prompt translation of Receivables to Cash. Our partners include NIBSS, E-Tranzact, CoralPay and various Commercial and Microfinance Banks.
